Quality Assurance

We check every aspect of your language project from initial conception to delivery. The Lingvotech Quality Assurance process ensures that final translations read as though originally written in the target language. In addition to translating and localizing all text, we check the functionality of interfaces; proofread for word selection, grammar, punctuation and flow; and conduct a cultural assessment to validate its appropriateness for the target market. Our independent proofreaders are trained professionals that analyze text for errors regarding spelling, hyphenation, mistranslation and omissions. Meaning and accuracy are maintained by thoroughly analyzing, editing and comparing the translated text against the original for aesthetic elements such as consistency in font, pagination, table of contents entries and index alphabetization, etc. Thus quality initiatives within the process consist of an accurate, complete and technically and culturally acceptable translation by a specialized, native speaking translator and a full edit of each translation by a second, independent, equally qualified linguist. The result is a document that is as effective in its new language as it was in its original form. Lingvotech guarantees the accuracy and precision of our work, supported by our warranty to make it right every time. If you are not satisfied with the quality of our work, Lingvotech will correct those pertinent areas until we have met your level of satisfaction.
