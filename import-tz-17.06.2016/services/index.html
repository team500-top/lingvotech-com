Services


The Lingvotech network consists of a number of professional linguists located in the target-language country who, working with this technology, provide nuanced translations that help clients achieve their communication goals.

We only use native translators who translate into their own languages and we use specialist translators for all technical translation texts. You can send us the work by e-mail, fax, mail or courier depending upon your needs, and we can return the translated documents electronically, or by disk and/or hard copy.

Translation Process

At Lingvotech, we understand that our clients are not only demanding quality translations, but also need to work with an efficient process so that schedules are not jeopardized. The processes and methodologies that we've developed over the years ensure that we deliver a final product that meets all these requirements.

When you confide a translation project to Lingvotech, you will receive a translation that is not only performed by a native speaker who maintains a regular connection with his or her home country, but who also has selected to focus on specific industries to ensure the most accurate interpretation and rendition of your corporate material.

Some of our clients choose to do basic translations in-house, however, to ensure their translations are market ready, we proofread/back-check and edit their work for them.

At times, these internal teams may not have the language skills or sophistication required and thus many clients come to Lingvotech to have us conduct final proofreading and editing checks.
